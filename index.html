About me

I received the B.S. degree in software engineering from Jinggangshan University in 2013. I worked for International Business Machines (IBM), ShenZhen Corporation as a java application developer in 2013. I received the M.Sc. degree in computer science from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen in 2018. Now, I am a PhD candadian in computer science from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen.
